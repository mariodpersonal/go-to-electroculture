# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## The Plain Version

We're a small, handcrafted copper shop — not a data company. We collect only what we need to get your order to your door and keep this shop running. We don't sell your information. We don't share it with anyone who doesn't need it to fulfill your order. Here are the details.

---

## What Information We Collect

### When You Place an Order
- Name
- Email address
- Shipping address
- Phone number (if provided for shipping purposes)

### When You Visit Our Website
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website
- IP address
- Device information

### When You Sign Up for Our Newsletter
- Email address

We do not collect payment card details directly. All payment processing is handled by PayPal (see below).

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Fulfill and ship your orders
- Communicate with you about your order status
- Send newsletter emails (only if you signed up)
- Improve our website and your shopping experience
- Respond to your questions and requests

We do not use your information for purposes unrelated to the above.

---

## Payment Processing

All payments are processed through **PayPal**. When you make a purchase, your payment information is collected and processed directly by PayPal — we never see or store your credit card or bank account details. PayPal's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at [paypal.com/privacy](https://www.paypal.com/privacy).

---

## Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small files stored on your device — to:

- Keep your shopping cart active during your visit
- Remember your preferences
- Understand how visitors use our site (analytics)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some website features may not function properly without them.

---

## Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. These tools collect anonymous, aggregated data about page views, traffic sources, and browsing behavior. This information helps us improve the site. Analytics data is not linked to your personal identity.

---

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share your information only with:

- **PayPal** — to process your payment
- **Shipping carriers** — to deliver your order (name and address only)
- **Email service providers** — to send order confirmations and newsletters (email only)

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

---

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** any inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your personal information
- **Opt out** of marketing emails at any time (every email includes an unsubscribe link)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our website or on Instagram **@unstoppable_one_leg_wonder**.

---

## Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
