# Returns Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## The Short Version

We stand behind everything we build. If something isn't right, we want to know about it. That said, these are handcrafted items made to order — so please read the details below before requesting a return.

## 30-Day Return Window

You have **30 days from the date of delivery** to request a return. To be eligible, the item must be:

- **Unused and in its original condition** — not placed in soil, not worn, not altered
- **In its original packaging**, if applicable
- **Free of damage caused after delivery**

Because every item is handcrafted by hand, minor variations in copper work, crystal placement, and finish are normal and not considered defects. Each piece is unique — that's the nature of handmade work.

## How to Start a Return

Contact us through our website contact form or on Instagram **@unstoppable_one_leg_wonder** with:

1. Your order number
2. The item(s) you'd like to return
3. The reason for the return

We'll respond within 2-3 business days with return instructions.

## Return Shipping

**The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs.** We recommend using a tracked shipping method — we can't be responsible for items lost in return transit.

## Refunds

Once we receive your return and confirm the item is in its original, unused condition, we'll process your refund within **5-7 business days**. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged from shipping or has a genuine defect in craftsmanship, contact us within **48 hours of delivery** with photos. We'll cover return shipping and send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice. We build these with care, and if something slipped through, we'll make it right.

## Items That Cannot Be Returned

- Items that have been used, placed in soil, worn, or altered
- Items damaged after delivery through misuse or mishandling
- Items returned after the 30-day window

## Questions?

We're not hiding behind a policy page. If you have a concern, reach out. We're real people who care about getting this right.
