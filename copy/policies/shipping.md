# Shipping Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## How Shipping Works

Every item in our shop is handcrafted and made to order. That means when you place an order, we start building it for you. This isn't a warehouse operation — it's a one-person workshop, and every piece gets the time and attention it deserves.

## Processing Time

Please allow **5 to 10 business days** for your order to be crafted and prepared for shipment. Some items — especially larger pyramids and multi-stone antennas — may take additional time due to the complexity of the build. If your order will take longer than 10 business days, we'll reach out and let you know.

## Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are **calculated by weight** at checkout. Rates are determined by the size and weight of your order and your delivery location.

## Delivery Area

We currently ship within the **United States**. If you're located outside the US and are interested in ordering, reach out to us on Instagram **@unstoppable_one_leg_wonder** and we'll see what we can work out.

## Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email so you can follow your package to your door.

## Shipping Damage

If your item arrives damaged during shipping, contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage and packaging. We'll work with you to make it right — whether that's a replacement or a refund. These are handcrafted items built with care, and we want them to reach you in the condition they left the workshop.

## Questions?

Reach out to us through our website contact form or on Instagram **@unstoppable_one_leg_wonder**. We're real people and we respond.
