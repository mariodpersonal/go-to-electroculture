# Design System Rules — Go To Electroculture

## Colors
- Primary: #B87333 (Burnished Copper) — CTAs, prices, accents
- Secondary: #4A5D3A (Garden Earth Green) — secondary buttons, badges
- Background: #F5F0E8 (Warm Stone) — NEVER use pure white (#FFF)
- Text: #2C2C2A (Charcoal Earth) — NEVER use pure black (#000)
- Surface: #EDE6D8 (Raw Canvas) — alternating sections, cards
- Iron: #4A4642 — footer, dark sections

## Typography
- Headings: DM Serif Display (serif, grounded)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (clean, readable)
- No script fonts. No handwritten fonts. The craft speaks through the product.

## Layout
- Mobile-first. Test at 375px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px.
- Generous whitespace between sections (py-16 to py-24).
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl).
- Border radius: 6-8px. Subtle, not bubbly.
- Shadows: Warm-toned (brown base rgba, not gray).

## Images
- Use next/image with unoptimized for external URLs.
- Placeholder images from Unsplash — always add REPLACE comments.
- 4:5 aspect ratio for product cards.
- Warm natural light. No studio strobes. No stock photo energy.

## Don'ts
- No gradients (except dark overlays on hero images).
- No neon or saturated colors.
- No pure white or pure black anywhere.
- No sacred geometry cliches or chakra diagrams in design.
